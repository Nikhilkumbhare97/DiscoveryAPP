The Discovery Land Comapny Foundation focuses on
broadening the scope of possibilities for disadvantaged childern
to lead productive lives.

Supporting the
community's children

It’s important that our private communities interact
with, and actively participate in, the wider
neighbourhood.

That’s why we established The Discovery Land
Company Foundation; which primarily supports
disadvantaged children in shelters and in foster care
and donates to carefully vetted, fully trusted charities.

It’s all about giving these kids a better start in life, and
giving them a little extra help to make the most of their
potential.
